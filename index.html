Reactor 79 is a strategy/simulation terminal game set in a 1970s-style nuclear power plant. The player manipulates plant equipment to stabilize the reactor and prevent disaster over 10 turns. Achieve the right RAD Score to win!

Game Instructions

  • Each turn, random events may occur affecting equipment.
  • Repair machinery by entering numbers 1-8 (or -1 to -8) or pass with 0.
  • Keep equipment within safe limits to avoid meltdown or collapse.
  • End of turn RAD Score sums all equipment values; score of 26 = win!
